Components list:
1. Arduino UNO or Arduino Nano
2. breadboard
3. Jumper Wire
4. 340 ohm or 1k Resistor
5. Led
6. 5 volt battery

connection:

Frist we connect led postive pin with resistor (1k or 340ohm) than resistor opposite pin  connect arduino digital pin 6.
led negative pin connect Arduino GND. 5 volt battery connect arduino vcc pin and GND pin connect arduino GND.
